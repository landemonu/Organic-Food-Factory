Our Story

Organic Food Factory started with a dream to provide fresh, pesticide-free food directly from farms to families. Our roots lie in sustainable agriculture, fair trade, and care for the environment. Today, we work with over 200 local farmers to ensure every product is fresh, nutritious, and chemical-free.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a global leader in providing organic, healthy, and sustainable food products that enhance the quality of life and protect the environment.

Our Mission

To empower farmers, promote organic farming practices, and deliver high-quality organic products to consumers while maintaining environmental balance.

Our Journey

2015

We started as a small community farm, selling fresh vegetables locally.

2017

Collaborated with local farmers and expanded our product range.

2020

Launched our first online organic store, reaching thousands of homes.

2023
